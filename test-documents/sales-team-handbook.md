## Sales Process

### Stage 1: Discovery Call (30 min)

**Goals**:
- Understand their current onboarding process
- Identify pain points
- Qualify as ICP
- Book demo

**Key Questions**:
1. "Walk me through your current onboarding process"
2. "How long does it typically take?"
3. "How many people are involved?"
4. "What's your biggest frustration with it?"
5. "What happens when internal tools or policies change?"
6. "How do employees find information after onboarding?"

### Stage 2: Demo (45 min)

**Demo Script**:

1. **Show the problem** (5 min)
   - "Traditional onboarding: generic, time-consuming, one-time event"
   
2. **Upload documents** (5 min)
   - Drag & drop company docs
   - Show processing in real-time
   - "This takes 30 seconds, not 30 hours"

3. **Ask questions** (15 min)
   - Live Q&A showing relevant answers
   - Demonstrate source citations
   - Show how it's specific to THEIR docs

4. **Show employer dashboard** (10 min)
   - Employee management
   - Document management
   - Analytics (coming soon)

5. **ROI Calculator** (10 min)
   - Input their numbers
   - Show cost savings
   - "This pays for itself in the first week"

**Demo Tips**:
- Use THEIR company name in examples
- Pre-upload sample docs for their industry
- Have backup plan if internet fails
- Leave 10 min for questions

### Stage 3: Pilot Proposal (Email)

**Pilot Structure**:
- 30-day free trial
- 10-20 users (1-2 teams)
- We help with doc upload
- Weekly check-ins
- Success metrics defined upfront

**Success Metrics**:
- Time to productivity (days)
- Questions asked per employee
- Satisfaction score (1-10)
- Cost savings calculated

## Handling Objections

### "We already have a knowledge base"

**Response**: "That's great! Most companies do. The difference is Werkly makes it conversational and role-specific. Instead of searching through hundreds of docs, employees just ask questions and get exactly what they need. Plus, it learns what people actually need to know based on what's being asked."

### "This seems expensive"

**Response**: "Let's do the math together. How much are you spending on onboarding now? [Calculate]. Most companies save 10x what they pay us. Want to see the ROI calculator?"

### "We need to think about it"

**Response**: "Totally understand. What specific concerns can I help address? [Pause for answer]. Also, what would make this a no-brainer for you?"

### "Our IT team needs to review security"

**Response**: "Absolutely! I'll send over our security documentation. We're SOC 2 compliant, use enterprise-grade encryption, and have RLS policies for data isolation. Happy to have our CTO join a call with your IT team."

### "We'll wait until next quarter"

**Response**: "I get it. Just so you know, the companies who start now save an average of $50K in Q1 alone. What if we started with a small pilot to prove ROI before next quarter's budget planning?"

## Competitive Intel

### Trainual

**Their Strength**: Easy to create training courses
**Their Weakness**: Static content, not AI-powered, not role-specific
**Our Edge**: "Trainual requires someone to build courses. Werkly automatically answers questions from your existing docs."

### Guru

**Their Strength**: Browser extension, Slack integration
**Their Weakness**: Manual knowledge curation, not AI-powered
**Our Edge**: "Guru requires manual tagging and categorization. Werkly uses AI to automatically understand and retrieve relevant information."

### Generic ChatGPT

**Their Strength**: Everyone knows it
**Their Weakness**: No company context, no data privacy
**Our Edge**: "ChatGPT doesn't know about YOUR company. Werkly is trained on YOUR docs, keeps YOUR data private, and gives YOUR-company-specific answers."
