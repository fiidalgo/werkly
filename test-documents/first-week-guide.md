## Common First-Week Questions

### "I feel overwhelmed"

**Normal!** Everyone feels this way. It gets easier. Talk to your buddy or manager.

### "I don't understand the codebase"

**Also normal!** It takes weeks to understand. Focus on your assigned area first.

### "I'm stuck and don't want to bother anyone"

**Please bother us!** Asking questions is expected and encouraged. Ping your buddy or #engineering.

### "I haven't written any code yet"

**That's OK!** Week 1 is mostly setup and learning. Code comes in week 2.

### "Should I be working at night to catch up?"

**No!** Don't work extra hours. Onboarding takes time. Sustainable pace is important.

## Your Onboarding Buddy

**Your buddy** is an experienced team member assigned to help you.

**Their role**:
- Answer questions (no question is too basic!)
- Pair program with you
- Review your first PRs
- Introduce you to the team
- Help navigate the culture

**Use them**:
- Slack DM anytime
- Schedule pairing sessions
- Get lunch/coffee together
- They're here for YOU
