# Benefits & Perks at Werkly

## Overview

We believe in taking care of our team. Here's everything you get as a Werkly employee.

## Health & Wellness

### Medical Insurance

**Coverage**: 100% premium covered for employees, 75% for dependents

**Providers**:
- **PPO**: Blue Cross Blue Shield
- **HMO**: Kaiser Permanente
- **HSA**: Available with high-deductible plan

**Enrollment**: Within 30 days of start date

### Dental & Vision

**Dental**: Delta Dental
- 2 cleanings per year (100% covered)
- Basic procedures (80% covered)
- Major procedures (50% covered)

**Vision**: VSP
- Annual eye exam (100% covered)
- $200 toward frames/contacts
- Discounts on LASIK

### Mental Health

- **Therapy**: 12 sessions per year (100% covered through Spring Health)
- **Meditation**: Headspace subscription (company paid)
- **Mental health days**: 4 per year (separate from PTO)

### Fitness

- **Gym reimbursement**: $100/month
- **Or**: ClassPass membership
- **Or**: Home gym equipment (up to $500/year)

## Financial Benefits

### Compensation

**Salary reviews**: Annually (every January)
**Performance bonuses**: Quarterly (based on OKRs)

### Equity

All employees receive stock options:
- **Offer letter**: Specifies # of options
- **Vesting**: 4 years (1 year cliff, then monthly)
- **Strike price**: Fair market value at grant date

**Example**:
- Granted: 10,000 options
- After 1 year: 2,500 vested (25%)
- After 2 years: 5,000 vested (50%)
- After 4 years: 10,000 vested (100%)

### 401(k)

**Employer match**: 4% (100% of first 4% you contribute)

**Example**:
- You contribute: 4% of salary ($4,000)
- Company contributes: $4,000
- Total: $8,000/year

**Vesting**: Immediate (all company contributions are yours)

### Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP)

Coming Q2 2025:
- Buy company stock at 15% discount
- Up to 10% of salary
- Quarterly purchase periods

## Leaving Werkly

### COBRA

If you leave, you can continue health insurance:
- Up to 18 months
- You pay full premium + 2% admin fee
- Must elect within 60 days

### 401(k) Rollover

Options:
- Leave with current provider
- Roll over to IRA
- Roll over to new employer's 401(k)

### Equity

- Vested options remain yours
- 90 days to exercise after leaving
- Consult financial advisor
