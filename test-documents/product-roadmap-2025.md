## Product Principles

### 1. Simple by Default

Don't overwhelm users with options. Smart defaults, progressive disclosure.

### 2. Fast & Reliable

- Response in < 2s
- 99.9% uptime
- Graceful degradation

### 3. Privacy First

- Company data isolation
- No cross-company leakage
- Clear data retention policies
- GDPR/SOC 2 compliant

### 4. Learn from Usage

- Anonymous usage analytics
- Identify common patterns
- Improve automatically
- Share insights with customers
