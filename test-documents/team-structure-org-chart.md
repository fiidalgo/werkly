## Team Culture

### Our Vibe

- **Collaborative**: We help each other
- **Transparent**: Share openly
- **High-trust**: Flexible hours, remote work
- **Fast-paced**: Startup energy
- **Low-ego**: No "rockstars", team sport

### What We Celebrate

- Shipping features
- Learning from failures
- Helping teammates
- Customer wins
- Personal milestones

### What We Avoid

- Office politics
- Blame culture
- Burnout culture
- Exclusivity
